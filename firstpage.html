Treatment

If anyone has symptoms such as fever, dry cough, shortness of breath or difficulty in breathing, and tiredness or fatigue, he/she should seek medical advice immediately to get tested for COVID-19.

COVID-19 positive people should adhere to medical advice on isolation at home or an institution for the safety of their family members and community at large.
